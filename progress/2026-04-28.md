# Progress 2026-04-28 — Sync con la tesis (LSHTM)

This file logs work that happened on 2026-04-28 in the **doctoral-thesis side**
of the project (private workspace), so the public repo has a paper trail of
what changed on the methodological chapter that this tutorial collection
supports.

## What changed in the thesis chapter

Chapter 1 (*State of the Art and Methodological Foundations of Multi-Omics
Integration for Asthma Phenotyping*, LSHTM thesis) received a substantive
refactor that synchronises every method section with the corresponding
tutorial in this repository.

- **New §1.4: chapter-tutorial mapping table.** Explicit one-to-one map
  between every method section in the chapter and the tutorial that
  demonstrates it on GS-BRCA: §2.1 → Tutorial 1 (sPLS-DA), §2.2 → Tutorial 2
  (DIABLO), §3.1–3.5 → Tutorial 3 (MOFA2), §4.3 → Tutorial 4 (SNF), §4.2 →
  Tutorial 5 (WGCNA), §5.1 → Tutorial 6 (VAE).
- **Notation pass.** Removed `\mathbf{}` notation throughout, aligning with
  Baião et al. 2025 and the tutorial collection.
- **Five intuition paragraphs** added before the equations of PCA, PLS,
  MOFA, SNF and VAE.
- **DIABLO subsection expanded** with the explicit design-matrix
  formulation $D_{m,m'}$ and the null-vs-full-design discussion that the
  tutorial walks through interactively.
- **MOFA prior treatment formalised**: Gaussian + Gamma hyperprior on
  precision $\alpha$, second-level spike-and-slab for feature-level
  sparsity. Direct mirror of the maths used in the MOFA2 tutorial.
- **WGCNA subsection** extended with the full TOM (Topological Overlap
  Measure) formula.
- **SNF subsection** extended with the convergence criterion and eigengap
  heuristic for cluster-count selection.
- **VAE subsection** now references PoE/MoE multi-modal extensions for
  the missing-block case.
- **§7.1 comparative perspective** anchored in benchmarking studies
  (Cantini et al. 2021 *Nat Comm*, Pierre-Jean et al. 2020 *Brief
  Bioinform*).
- **13 new references** (refs 56–68 in chapter): Cantini 2021, Pierre-Jean
  2020, Picard 2021, Lock 2013 (JIVE), Bishop 1999 (Bayesian PCA / ARD),
  Velten 2022 (MEFISTO), Yip & Horvath 2007 (TOM), Reel 2021, Shi 2019
  (PoE/MoE VAE), Allesøe 2023 (MOVE), Stein-O'Brien 2018, Vahabi &
  Michailidis 2022, Ulfenborg 2019.

## What does NOT change in this repository

The tutorials in this repository (`02_splsda/` through `07_vae/`, plus
`08_comparison/`) are unchanged by this sync. They already reflect the
methodological content the chapter now references; the chapter was the
side that was lagging.
